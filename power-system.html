Power System

Details for the power architecture and battery support in the ESP32-S3 CAM AI eBook Reader.

Power Architecture

  • 3.3V logic supply for ESP32-S3, display, camera, and audio peripherals.
  • Separate power rails for analog and digital circuits when possible.
  • Battery connector and optional charging module support.
  • Power filtering for the speaker amplifier and SD card interface.

Battery and Charging

For portable operation, use a LiPo battery with protection and charger circuitry. Ensure the battery voltage is regulated to 3.3V for the ESP32-S3 and associated peripherals.
