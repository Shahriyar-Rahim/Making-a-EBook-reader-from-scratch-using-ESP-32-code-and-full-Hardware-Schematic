Breadboard

This page covers the breadboard layout for prototyping the ESP32-S3 CAM AI eBook Reader hardware before moving to a permanent PCB.

Recommended Breadboard Setup

  • Mount the ESP32-S3 CAM module on a stable base or adapter board.
  • Keep power rails clearly separated for 3.3V and GND.
  • Use short jumper wires for SPI and I2S connections.
  • Place the display and touch signals near the ESP32-S3 to reduce noise.

Prototyping Tips

  1. Verify power and ground connections before adding devices.
  2. Test the camera interface separately before integrating the display.
  3. Check I2S wiring with a simple audio playback sketch.
  4. Secure the microphone and speaker to avoid mechanical noise.
