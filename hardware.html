Hardware

This section describes the hardware components used in the ESP32-S3 CAM AI eBook Reader, including the microcontroller, sensors, audio peripherals, display, and power system.

Core Controller

  • ESP32-S3 CAM module
  • Dual-core Xtensa 32-bit MCU
  • Built-in camera connector and Wi-Fi/Bluetooth radios

Display

  • SPI-based color TFT display
  • Touchscreen controller (XPT2046)
  • Integrated LVGL UI support for responsive touch menus

Camera

  • ESP32-S3 CAM camera module
  • Supports image capture for OCR and visual data
  • Connected through the onboard camera interface

Audio

  • INMP441 digital MEMS microphone
  • MAX98357A I2S audio amplifier
  • SD card based WAV recording and playback

Power

Power is managed by the main board with a battery connector, optional charger support, and protection circuitry. The design aims for portability and reasonable runtime under mixed display, camera, and audio operation.
