Troubleshooting

Resolve common issues with the ESP32-S3 CAM AI eBook Reader hardware and firmware.

Display Issues

  • Check display power and SPI connections.
  • Verify the correct display driver pins are configured in firmware.
  • Confirm the touch controller is wired to the same SPI bus.

Camera Issues

  • Ensure the camera ribbon is seated correctly.
  • Use proper lighting for OCR captures.
  • Test camera and boot logs separately to isolate initialization failures.

Audio Issues

  • Verify I2S microphone and speaker wiring.
  • Inspect SD card formatting to ensure WAV file writes succeed.
  • Check firmware logs for audio engine state changes.
