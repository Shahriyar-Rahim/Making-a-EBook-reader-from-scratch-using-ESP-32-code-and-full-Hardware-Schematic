Wiring

This page describes the wiring connections for the ESP32-S3 CAM AI eBook Reader, including the display, touch controller, camera, audio, and SD card.

SPI Display

  • Display SCK to ESP32-S3 SPI clock pin
  • Display MOSI to ESP32-S3 SPI MOSI pin
  • Display CS to a dedicated chip select pin
  • Display DC and RESET to GPIO control pins

Touch Controller

  • XPT2046 CS to GPIO
  • XPT2046 CLK, DIN, DOUT to shared SPI lines
  • Touch IRQ to a GPIO interrupt pin

Camera

Connect the camera ribbon to the ESP32-S3 CAM module socket. Ensure the connector is fully seated and the ribbon is oriented correctly.

SD Card

SD card signals use SPI or SDMMC depending on board support. Wire CS, MISO, MOSI, and CLK from the SD socket to the ESP32-S3 bus, and connect power and ground securely.

Audio

  • INMP441 microphone connected to I2S input pins
  • MAX98357A speaker amplifier connected to I2S output pins
  • Power amplifier with 3.3V and GND
