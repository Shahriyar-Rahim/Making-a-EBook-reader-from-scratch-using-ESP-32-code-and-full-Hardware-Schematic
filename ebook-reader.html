eBook Reader

This page documents the reader experience built on the ESP32-S3 CAM AI eBook Reader firmware, including text navigation, display controls, and reader workflow.

Reader Workflow

  • Load text or EPUB files from the SD card.
  • Navigate pages with touch controls or buttons.
  • Use theme and font size settings for comfortable reading.
  • Track reading progress and call AI actions from within the reader.

Text Rendering

The system renders book text with LVGL labels on the TFT display. The UI supports page boundaries, line wrapping, and text scaling for readability.

Bookmarks and Notes

Bookmarks and notes can be captured while reading. Notes may later be reviewed through the AI assistant or saved to the SD card for study review.
