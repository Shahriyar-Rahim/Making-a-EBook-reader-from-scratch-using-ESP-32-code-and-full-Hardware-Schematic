Bill of Materials

This page lists the key parts for building the ESP32-S3 CAM AI eBook Reader, including core modules and recommended accessories.

Essential Parts

  • ESP32-S3 CAM module
  • SPI TFT display with XPT2046 touch controller
  • MicroSD card socket or adapter
  • INMP441 digital microphone
  • MAX98357A I2S audio amplifier or speaker module

Recommended Accessories

  • LiPo battery and charger board
  • Standoffs or mounting hardware for the camera module
  • Header connectors and jumper wires
  • Spare SD cards and protective enclosure
