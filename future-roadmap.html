Future Roadmap

This page outlines planned enhancements for the ESP32-S3 CAM AI eBook Reader, focusing on audio, AI, OCR, and user experience improvements.

Planned Enhancements

  • Full audiobook mode with audio book playback and book text synchronization
  • Text-to-speech and speech-to-text support
  • Improved OCR accuracy and page scanning workflows
  • Expanded cloud AI capabilities and local caching

UX Improvements

Better reader controls, annotations, bookmarks, and a more polished mobile-friendly UI are planned for later iterations.

Hardware Revisions

Future hardware revisions may include more compact board designs, integrated battery charging, and optimized audio amplifier layouts for consistent sound quality.
