PCB

This page outlines the PCB design considerations for the ESP32-S3 CAM AI eBook Reader, including board stack, component placement, and signal routing.

Design Goals

  • Compact footprint for the ESP32-S3 CAM module and display connector.
  • Clear separation of digital, analog, and power domains.
  • Robust ground plane to support SD card, audio, and Wi-Fi RF performance.
  • Accessible headers for programming, debugging, and expansion.

Routing Guidelines

Keep high-speed traces short and avoid crossing between domains. Route SPI, I2S, and SD lines with matched lengths where possible, and place bypass capacitors close to power pins.
