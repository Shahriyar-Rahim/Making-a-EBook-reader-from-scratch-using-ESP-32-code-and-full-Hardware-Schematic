Project Overview

The ESP32-S3 CAM AI eBook Reader is a purpose-built integrated reading device combining text display, a camera for OCR, voice note recording, cloud AI assistance, and Wi-Fi networking. It is designed for learners, students, and professionals who need a compact, offline-capable reading tool with intelligent study support.

Why this project exists

This project addresses the gap between standard eReaders and experimental AI devices by integrating a complete hardware-software platform around the ESP32-S3 CAM. It adds cloud AI assistance, offline voice note capture, and OCR scanning while preserving the portability and low cost of embedded hardware.

  • Self-contained eBook reader with AI-enhanced study tools
  • Camera-based text capture and scanning workflow
  • Voice note recording and playback
  • Expandable TTS/STT architecture for future audio books

Target Users

  • Students who study with digital textbooks and need summarization
  • Researchers who want fast note capture with OCR and cloud AI
  • Language learners who want vocabulary, quizzes, and reading assistance
  • Developers and makers who want a platform for embedded AI experiments

Use Cases

eBook Reader

Read text files and EPUBs using a responsive LVGL interface with theme and font controls.

AI Assistant

Summarize content, generate study questions, and chat with the book context through cloud AI.

OCR Scanner

Capture text from paper pages with the ESP32-S3 camera and process it into editable text.

Digital Notebook

Save notes, voice clips, and transcriptions to SD storage for review and research.

System Highlights

  1. ESP32-S3 CAM core handles display, camera, networking, and AI interfacing.
  2. LVGL-based UI on a color SPI display with touch input and reader controls.
  3. Voice note recording via INMP441 mic and playback through MAX98357A amplifier.
  4. Cloud-enabled AI integrations for summarization, notes, and Q&A.
